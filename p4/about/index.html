The Pot, Tool

From thier 4th studio album "10,000 days", The Pot is a song about hypocrisy. At the time of release the band consisted of Danny Carey (drums), Justin Chancellor (bass), Adam Jones (guitar), and Maynard James Keenan (vocals).
